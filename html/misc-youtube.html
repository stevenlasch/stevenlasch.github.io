Reading Stages

  1. Consumption: Everything you consume must be digested.
  2. Digestive: This has to be balanced against the consumption.

Not all information is equal. What goes into your brain is less important than what stays in your brain

PACER Acronym

  1. Procedural: Any information on how something is executed. The key is practice.
  2. Analogous: When something is related to something you all ready know. The key is to critique how good the analogy is
  3. Conceptual: Facts and information. The key is mapping this information. Non-linear network like note taking.
  4. Evidence: Information to prove a conceptual point. The key is to store and rehearse this.
  5. Reference: The key is to store and rehearse this.
